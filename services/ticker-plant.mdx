AlgoSeek's third-generation ticker plant processes raw PCAP data and exchange feeds from all major US stock exchanges, normalizing them into clean, queryable market data. Available as a managed cloud service or an on-premises deployment, it eliminates the substantial cost and operational complexity of building and maintaining your own exchange connectivity infrastructure — so your engineering team can focus on what happens after the data arrives.

## What the Ticker Plant Does

- Connects to and processes data feeds from all major US stock exchanges
- Normalizes raw PCAPs into structured, standardized trade and quote records
- Calculates the National Best Bid and Offer (NBBO), LULD price bands, Short Sale Rule (SSR) flags, and regulatory halt states in real time
- Stores processed data in a row-based SQL-queryable database for immediate downstream use
- Outputs data via multicast, TCP socket, WebSocket, RESTful API, or Kafka streaming
- Supports time-machine feed replay for strategy testing, compliance review, and disaster recovery scenarios

## Output Methods

| Output | Protocol | Best For |
|---|---|---|
| Multicast | UDP | Market data distribution to multiple simultaneous consumers |
| TCP Socket | TCP | Reliable point-to-point data feeds with delivery guarantees |
| WebSocket | WS / WSS | Browser-based applications and real-time web interfaces |
| REST API | HTTP | Request-driven data access and ad hoc queries |
| Kafka Streaming | Kafka | Event-driven microservices and high-throughput streaming pipelines |
