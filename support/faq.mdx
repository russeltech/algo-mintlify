This page answers the most common questions from AlgoSeek subscribers. If you don't find your answer here, contact [support@algoseek.com](mailto:support@algoseek.com) and the support team will respond promptly.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What is the history depth of AlgoSeek data?">
    AlgoSeek data goes back to **2007** for most US equity, options, and futures datasets. Specific start dates vary by dataset — check the dataset detail page in the Console or contact [support@algoseek.com](mailto:support@algoseek.com) for confirmation on a particular product.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What time zone are timestamps in?">
    All timestamps are in **Eastern Time (ET)**. See the [Timestamps guide](/concepts/timestamps) for details on precision levels (millisecond, microsecond, nanosecond) and bar open/close conventions.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How quickly is daily data available after market close?">
    Daily files are typically available **2–4 hours after market close**. For end-of-day datasets, this means data is generally available by approximately **8–10 PM ET**. If a file has not arrived by 10 PM ET, contact [support@algoseek.com](mailto:support@algoseek.com) with your subscription ID and dataset name.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What is the difference between TAQ and Trade Only datasets?">
    **TAQ (Trade and Quote)** datasets include both trade prints and top-of-book quote updates, giving you full NBBO context for every moment of the trading day. **Trade Only** datasets contain only executed trade prints — lower data volume, simpler structure, and suitable for use cases where quote-level detail is not required, such as volume analysis or transaction cost estimation.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What is FINRA/TRF data and should I exclude it?">
    FINRA TRF (Trade Reporting Facility) records cover trades executed off public exchanges — including dark pools, broker-dealer internalization, and block trades. Because the reporting window allows up to a **10-second delay**, these prints can appear at prices outside the prevailing NBBO and at timestamps that do not reflect actual execution time.

    For backtests that rely on exchange-only price discovery, use the **"Excluding FINRA/TRF"** dataset variants to avoid distortion from off-exchange prints.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How does ArdaDB pricing work?">
    **ArdaDB Basic** is billed at a fixed monthly fee. **ArdaDB Enterprise** is billed based on actual compute usage, making it cost-efficient for workloads with variable query intensity. No additional charges are applied for storage beyond standard cloud provider fees. Contact [sales@algoseek.com](mailto:sales@algoseek.com) for current pricing tiers and volume discounts.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I bring my own data into ArdaDB?">
    Yes. ArdaDB supports importing data from a wide range of sources and formats, including:

    - **Databases:** PostgreSQL, BigQuery, Redshift, Snowflake, MongoDB
    - **File formats:** CSV, TSV, JSON, Parquet

    Your imported data is stored in dedicated cloud storage provisioned for your account, kept separate from the shared AlgoSeek data catalog while remaining queryable through the same ArdaDB interface.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I handle corporate actions in backtests?">
    Use the **Detailed Adjustment Factors** or **Basic Adjustment Factors** datasets to construct adjusted price series from raw unadjusted data. Alternatively, subscribe to one of the pre-adjusted dataset variants if you prefer to receive split- and dividend-adjusted prices directly. See the [Corporate Actions guide](/concepts/corporate-actions) for worked examples of both approaches.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What formats are available for file delivery?">
    AlgoSeek supports the following delivery formats:

    - **Columnar / binary:** Parquet, HDF5, Feather / Arrow
    - **Text-based:** CSV, TSV, JSON
    - **Financial:** FIX
    - **Spreadsheet:** Excel (.xlsx)

    Contact [support@algoseek.com](mailto:support@algoseek.com) to configure your preferred format or to request a format not listed here.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is global (non-US) data available?">
    Global market data coverage is currently in development. Contact [sales@algoseek.com](mailto:sales@algoseek.com) to register your interest and be notified when international datasets become available.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
