## What AlgoSeek Offers

AlgoSeek sources data directly from the SIP consolidated feed via co-located ticker plant servers in Equinix NY2 and NY4 data centers. Coverage spans the full US market — all 15+ exchanges and marketplaces — and the archive goes back to 2007.
