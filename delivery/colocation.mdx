AlgoSeek operates a third-generation ticker plant co-located at Equinix NY2 and NY4, directly connected to all major US exchanges via 10Gb fiber. Co-location access gives you sub-15-microsecond market data suitable for high-frequency trading, market making, and latency-sensitive strategies — with a zero-downtime track record since inception.

## Access Methods

AlgoSeek supports three connectivity models. Choose based on your latency requirements, infrastructure footprint, and operational preferences.

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Multicast" icon="tower-broadcast">
    Broadcast data streams ideal for consuming multiple instruments simultaneously. One feed serves your entire subscriber universe with no per-symbol connection overhead.
  </Card>
  <Card title="TCP Socket" icon="plug">
    Point-to-point connection with reliable, ordered delivery for individual feeds. Best when you need guaranteed sequencing and per-symbol subscription control.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Hosted Solution" icon="server">
    AlgoSeek manages servers in the data center so you connect remotely. Eliminates the need for your own co-location hardware while preserving ultra-low-latency access.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## What the Feed Provides

The co-location feed gives you comprehensive, exchange-native market data across the full US equity market structure:

- Full top-of-book and depth-of-book quotes from all US exchanges
- Trade prints with exchange flags, condition codes, and aggressor identification
- NBBO calculation and LULD price bands
- Real-time short sale restriction (SSR) and regulatory halt flags
- Feed replay capability for testing and missed-data recovery
- Dynamic ticker subscription management (add/remove symbols at runtime)

## Getting Connected

<Steps>
  <Step title="Contact AlgoSeek Sales">
    Reach out to [sales@algoseek.com](mailto:sales@algoseek.com) to discuss co-location requirements, available capacity, and which access method fits your architecture.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign the Co-Location Agreement">
    Once terms are confirmed, sign the co-location agreement. AlgoSeek will provision your connection and provide endpoint details, credentials, and network configuration instructions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect Your Systems">
    Point your systems at the provisioned feed endpoint. AlgoSeek provides sample client code for both TCP and multicast protocols along with hands-on integration support during onboarding.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Validate with Feed Replay">
    Use the feed replay function to test your implementation against historical data before going live. Replay lets you verify message handling, ordering logic, and reconnection behavior in a controlled environment.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Co-location access requires a signed agreement and dedicated hardware provisioning. Lead time is typically 5–10 business days. Contact sales early to avoid delays in your go-live timeline.
</Warning>
